Why facilities choose Synergy Podiatry

At synergy podiatry, we understand the specific needs and challenges of aged care. Our services are designed to integrate seamlessly into your facility’s operations, providing consistent, high-quality podiatry care that enhances residents’ comfort, mobility, and overall well-being.

Our approach focuses not only on routine foot care but also on early identification of high-risk conditions such as diabetic foot ulcers, pressure injuries, and mobility issues — helping prevent complications and hospitalisations.
