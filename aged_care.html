Why facilities choose Synergy Podiatry

At synergy podiatry, we understand the specific needs and challenges of aged care. Our services are designed to integrate seamlessly into your facility’s operations, providing consistent, high-quality podiatry care that enhances residents’ comfort, mobility, and overall well-being.

Our approach focuses not only on routine foot care but also on early identification of high-risk conditions such as diabetic foot ulcers, pressure injuries, and mobility issues — helping prevent complications and hospitalisations.

Reliable podiatry care you can trust, with clarity and compliance every step

Consistent weekly service

We have maintained a regular weekly visit schedule for over two years — with zero missed weeks — at major facilities including Regis Aged Care and Catholic Health Australia. Your residents can rely on ongoing care without disruption.

Experienced & AHPRA-registered podiatrists

Our podiatrists are fully AHPRA-registered with extensive aged care experience. We ensure each clinician follows best practice in geriatric foot care, infection control, and documentation.

Clear communication with care teams

We communicate directly with your clinical team, RNs, and care coordinators to ensure that every resident’s podiatry needs are addressed in line with care plans. We also liaise with families and GPs where appropriate.

Compliant & audit-ready documentation

Our documentation is designed to meet aged care compliance standards and supports facility care planning, ACFI/AN-ACC, and accreditation audits.
